Pace Scenario Breakdown
Model uses Beyer/Brisnet speed figures, E1/E2/LP pace figures, CWP score, DI, post position historical win rates, running style, and trainer/jockey multipliers. Each simulation adds normally-distributed random variance (σ≈8 Beyer pts for speed, ±2 positions for trip luck). Results show probability distributions, not predictions. Favorites win ~30% of Derbies historically — the model reflects this. International horses (N/A CWP) are included with speed-figure estimates only.
Running styles: E=Front-runner · EP=Early presser · S=Sustained closer · C=Deep closer  ·  Fig trend: ↑ improving · → stable · ↓ regressing  ·  Pace figures (Brisnet): E1=early speed (first ¼M) · E2=middle pace (first ½M) · LP=late pace (final ⅜) · Higher=faster · Green≥108 · Amber≥98 · Red below 98 for LP  ·  ½M call: actual clockings from most recent prep (sub-:46 is hot, :48+ is moderate/slow) · Final ⅛: sub-12.00s is exceptional closing speed  ·  Pedigree colors: Green=KY Derby winner · Gold=Triple Crown winner · Blue=Chef-de-Race · Orange=Inbreeding  ·  Sources: BloodHorse · TDN · TwinSpires · DRF · Secretariat.com · America's Best Racing · Total Performance Data · Paulick Report
